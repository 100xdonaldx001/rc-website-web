LAND YOUR DREAM JOB


You can drive a random selection of over 200 vehicles from three categories: Performance, Heavy, and Aviation.

Transporter Runs

Transporters will spawn a variety of vehicles, including muscle, sports, and super cars. There are even a few surprises in the ultra-rare Amazing class of vehicles.

Heavy Transport

Trucking enthusiasts will love our Heavy Transport runs. These include everything from box trucks to randomized semi-cab & trailer combinations.

Aviator

If you enjoy flying, you'll need to check out Aviator. This profession has a chance to spawn nearly every non-heavy aircraft in Grand Theft Auto, including helicopters.

Professional

This exclusive profession is granted a special license. You'll be able to use every spawner at your leisure with no limitations. The barrier of entry is high and so are the payouts.
